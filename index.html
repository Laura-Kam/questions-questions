General Questions

How long does it take to learn JS?

This question is difficult to answer. I will give you two case studies. If you have the brain capacity to study for five hours a day, and are not full-time employed. You can learn within a year or so.

If you are working full time and can manage two hours, three times a week, a year an a half is more realistic to be able to achieve a junior role.

Am I smart enough to learn?

There of course needs to be a strong level of ability in terms of logic and maths. You however, do not need to be an absolute genius to be a coder. Hard work, commitment and a growth mindset are key to becoming a succesful developer.

How can I learn to code cheaply and effectively?

I highly recommend Udemy courses by John Smilga. You will benefit from clear manageable explanations, with useful incremental practice. He also has a miriad of mini-projects to get your teeth into, without being overwhelming.
